# Apexlog — GPS &amp; Motion Telemetry

*[日本語](index.ja) · [User Manual](manual) · [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy)*

**Turn your phone into a vehicle telemetry logger.** Apexlog records your GPS
track and high-rate motion data while you ride or drive, then lets you replay
and analyze every run on a map — with a G-G friction circle and fused vehicle
attitude (lean, pitch, heading).

Built for motorcycles, cars, karts, and track days. **Private by design: your
recordings stay on your device.**

---

## What it does

**Record**
- GPS track + ~100&nbsp;Hz IMU (accelerometer + gyroscope) to a simple, open
  NDJSON file. Sensor rates are adjustable to save battery.
- **Travel mode:** a low-power journey logger (On foot / Vehicle) that writes a
  point every few metres — log a whole trip day, not just a session.
- **Background recording (Pro):** keep logging with the screen off or your
  navigation app on top — made for the bike, where the nav owns the screen.

**Replay &amp; analyze**
- Your ride drawn on an OpenStreetMap track.
- **G-G diagram** (friction circle) showing combined longitudinal/lateral G.
- A switchable **vehicle gauge**: top-down (G + yaw), rear-view (lean),
  side-view (pitch) — or a **journey gauge** (activity icon + trip odometer)
  for travel logs.
- Speed, lean, pitch and a sensor-fused heading along a scrubbable timeline.

**Own your data**
- Recordings are written to an **open NDJSON format** you can export and parse
  yourself — see the [data-format reference](manual#data-format-reference).
- **GPX / KML export (Pro):** open your route in Google My Maps, Google Earth,
  Strava and other GPS tools.
- No account, no cloud, no analytics, no tracking.
