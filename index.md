## What it does

**Record**
- GPS track + up to 100&nbsp;Hz IMU (accelerometer + gyroscope) to a simple, open NDJSON file. Sensor rates are adjustable to save battery.
- **Travel mode:** a point every few metres — log a whole trip day, not just a session.
- **Background recording (Pro):** keep logging with the screen off or your navigation app on top — made for the bike, where the nav owns the screen.

**Replay & analyze**
- Your ride on an OpenStreetMap track, with a scrubbable timeline.
- **G-G diagram** (friction circle), switchable **vehicle gauge** — top-down (G + yaw), rear (lean), side (pitch) — or a **journey gauge** for travel logs.
- **Harsh-event detection:** braking / cornering / acceleration events as tappable map markers.

**Own your data**
- Open **NDJSON** format you can parse yourself — see the [data-format reference](manual#data-format-reference).
- **GPX / KML export (Pro):** open your route in Google My Maps, Google Earth, Strava and more.
- No account, no cloud, no analytics, no tracking.
